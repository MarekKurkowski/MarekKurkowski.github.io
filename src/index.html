About us

We are an artistic metalwork smithy located in the heart of Gdansk shipyard. We make two realities meet: the world of art and the world of business. This lets us deliver personalized, prestigious products of the highest quality worldwide. Always on time.

Our blacksmiths are the leaders on the Polish market, with over 15 years hands-on experience and numerous awards in their portfolio, from National Art of Blacksmith Fairs in Wojciechow and many others prestigious competitions carried out, among others, in the House of Parliament, Westminster Abbey or Royal Castle in Warsaw. Each of those outstanding artists and experts holds a master diploma with the highest grades.

We do not only create railings, gates, fences, artistic projects, small external architecture, furniture, apartment's equipment. We also help in reconstructions and maintenance of monuments or sculptures - from modern projects with glass or wood to classical architectural blacksmithing.
